Step 1 First Select your vehicle:

Find the suitable best Japanese vehicle for you using our search function or from our website. Once you find the used car you want, please check its details and photos. Even you can see the auction sheet. Use the quote function (select your country) to immediately estimate the total cost from Japan to your Country. (Online quotation is not available for some Country / vehicle combinations). So please contact us from the “Contact” page

Step 2 Get a free invoice and order the vehicle:

Request for an invoice for any used car from our stock. That is free and easy. Input your details and we will email you an invoice within some time. If you are deciding to purchase, contact us to reserve the vehicle for you before someone else buys it.

Step 3 Complete the payment:

Complete the payment by Bank Telegraphic Transfer, from your country bank. You can pay us by using the PayPal system. After you have completed the payment then please send us a copy of Telegraphic Transfer copy as proof of payment. When we will get the payment copy, then we will email you a confirmation.

Step 4 Your vehicle is shipped:

After receiving the full payment we will immediately start the export arrangements for the first ship available. You can follow up the status of your shipment by us, at any time. We will send the all required documents to you or your agent by DHL courier a few days after the vessel leaves Japan.

Step 5 Collect your vehicle.

Once you receive those documents, you can start arranging collection and registration in your country of the vehicle. A custom(cleaning) agent can help you to complete the procedure and give you an indication on the fee and taxes to be paid. After completing the procedures, drive your vehicle to your home!
