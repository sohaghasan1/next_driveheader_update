About Us

At NEXT DRIVE CO., LTD, we specialize in exporting high-quality Japanese used cars worldwide, operating from Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. Our mission is to make owning a Japanese vehicle both affordable and hassle-free, providing unmatched convenience and exceptional service.

Our Mission
At Next Drive Co., Ltd, our mission is to provide an unparalleled customer experience through fast, reliable shipment of top-quality vehicles. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standards of automotive excellence, ensuring each car meets rigorous quality benchmarks. By offering personalized service tailored to the unique needs of our clients, we aim to make every purchase seamless, efficient, and enjoyable. We strive to exceed expectations with our commitment to speed, quality, and customer-centric solutions.

Our Vision
To revolutionize the way customers access their dream cars from Japan, providing smooth and efficient service while building lasting global connections.
